03 : About me

Computer Scientist

As a junior at the University of Houston - Clear Lake studying computer science, my passion for technology and programming has only continued to grow. I've worked on various projects, honing my skills in Python, Java, and web design, and I'm eager to apply them to real-world challenges. My ultimate goal is to work as a full-stack developer and to explore the fascinating world of machine learning. I'm constantly seeking out new opportunities to expand my knowledge and skillset, and I'm particularly excited about the prospect of landing an internship at an innovative and forward-thinking company where I can contribute my talents and learn from industry leaders.

Artist

My art is a dynamic and multidisciplinary fusion of technology and creativity that transcends traditional boundaries. As a seasoned graphic designer, filmmaker, video editor, and photographer with over six years of experience, I bring a wealth of skills and perspective to every project. By combining my technical expertise with my artistic vision, I am able to craft designs and visual media that are at once cutting-edge and timeless. From bold graphics to stunning cinematography, my work is a testament to the limitless possibilities of art and technology. It's a forward-thinking approach that is sure to captivate and inspire.

Personal

I am a driven and multi-talented individual with a diverse skill set and a passion for the arts and technology. With an associate's degree in art from the College of the Mainland and currently pursuing a bachelor's in computer science at the University of Houston - Clear Lake with a 3.741 GPA, I have a strong academic background in both creative and technical fields. I've gained experience as a customer service associate at Walgreens and currently work as a computer lab assistant at UHCL. I have a deep love for film and graphic design, which I explore both independently and through my role as the Graphic Design Officer for my school's Art Association. Above all, I'm a collaborative spirit who relishes the opportunity to work with individuals from diverse backgrounds to bring ideas to life.
